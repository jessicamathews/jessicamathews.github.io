| Pay It Forward Program |

Through our new Pay It Forward program, people have the opportunity to take it a step further. You can either purchase a planner (at a discounted rate) for someone in need or pledge to receive a planner for free if you cannot afford one.

Here's how it works:

  1. Someone who cannot afford a planner signs a pledge to become a potential recipient here.
  2. A sponsor buys a planner at a discounted price for a recipient. (Passion Planner will take care of shipping costs!)
  3. Recipient gets a planner with his or her sponsor's name and email address
  4. Recipient will then send a thank you letter at the beginning and end of the year.
  5. The recipient is encouraged to become a future sponsor.

I believe in this program so much that I will match the first 500 sponsorships, meaning you will be sponsoring 2 people for the price of one.
